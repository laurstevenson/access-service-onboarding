Hi there!

Welcome to the AA101 Workshop! This online workshop is for all students with a medical condition or disability, which may be temporary or permanent, who want to learn general information about Academic Accommodations. This workshop is to be completed prior to the first appointment with a Learning specialist. Workshop topics include disability definitions, introduce some of the academic accommodations we offer, the regulations and processes we follow and more.

Should I do this workshop?

University of Ottawa students with disabilities must complete this workshop before registering for academic accommodations. Is this workshop for me? Take the following checklist to see if this workshop applies to you!

  • I am or will enroll at University of Ottawa
  • I have or will confirm that I have a disability
  • I have or will get disability documentations
  • I have or will open my Ventus account
  • I have or will complete the intake questionnaire
  • I have or will book an appointment to meet a learning specialist

Learning Objectives

During this workshop you will learn about:

  1. Disability definitions and types
  2. Academic Accommodations
  3. Roles and responsibilities: Learning specialist, Professor, Student
  4. Types of accommodation plans: Teaching, Transcriptions, Learning Strategies, Exams
  5. Registering for accommodations
  6. Campus resources

Academic Accommodations

Goal of the Accommodation Process

To put students on an equal playing field. The student should still feel challenged with their studies but not to the point where things are impossible to access. The academic accommodation plan put in place at the University of Ottawa may be different from the one in high school or another institution. Academic Regulation I-16

Regulation I.16 intends to provide guidance for students, faculty, and staff to understand the process by which accommodations for students with disabilities are made at the University.

This regulation:

  • clearly defines the responsibilities of the key University members involved in the process of accommodation
  • expands upon and clarifies the scope of this regulation
  • outlines the key principles to be considered when determining and implementing accommodations

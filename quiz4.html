Imagine that you are feeling 100%, then something unexpected happens. In order to defer an exam or assignment due to medical/unpexpected situations, what kind of scenarios might require you to talk to your doctor right away?

Reveal answer!

There are many different reasons why you may need to contact your healthcare provider and defer an exam/assignment, some examples include:

  • You realize you are having symptoms of a cold or flu and maybe even a fever
  • Sudden symptoms or signs that you are not thinking clearly and feel a panic or anxiety attack.
  • The assignment you worked on is due tomorrow but you slipped and hit your head so hard that you might have a concussion.
  • The symptoms of your medical condition have changed, worsened or become very painful such as a migraine or chronic illness.

Whatever your situation may be, refer to Academic Regulation 9.5 to follow the university's guideline's on deferring an exam or requesting an extension on an assignment.

True or False: My Learning specialist can talk with my professor to excuse me from writing my exam.

Reveal answer!
False!

Although Learning Specialists are here to support you, the academic accommodation process is a shared responsibility. When it comes to Academic Regulation 9.5, you are responsible to communicate with your professors and to provide supportng documentation to justify your absence. Your Learning Specialist will explain this regulation and answer any questions you may have about it.

What percentage of the world's population suffer from a disability?

Reveal answer!
15%

15 % of the world population live with disability. This would correspond to more than one billion of people! Between 110 million (2.2%) and 190 million (3.8%) people 15 years and older have significant difficulties in functioning

True or False? Disabilities are always visible and easy to identify.

Reveal answer!
False!

Disabilities are often invisible and are place in extremely diverse categories. Individual living with a disability may have physical illness (ex. injury, epilepsy, visual impairment...), a mental impairment or developmental disorder (ex. Attention Deficit Disorder, Autism Disorder), a learning disability (ex. Non-verbal learning disability, dyslexia, dyscalculia) or mental health condition (Ex. Depression, Anxiety, Obsessive Compulsive Disorder...).

True of False? Disabilities may be considered permanent or temporary.

Reveal answer!
True!

Certain disabilities are permanent, meaning that they are expected to remain with the person for life, while other disabilities are temporary and are expected to resolve or improve over time. Permanent conditions can be categorized as chronic, degenerative and/or episodic. Disabilities can be visible or invisible to others. Unfortunately, invisible as well as visible symptoms can result in misunderstandings as well as judgements.
